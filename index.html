Platform Features

Everything in One Place

Eight powerful wellness tools, each on its own page, built for simplicity and rural access.

Wellness Assessment

Multi-factor wellness scoring: BMI, waist ratio, activity level, and age — all in one intuitive dashboard.

BMI Calculator

Dedicated calculator with gauge visualization, metric/imperial toggle, and healthy weight range display.

Emergency Response

Animated emergency button simulation demonstrating rural community rapid-response systems.

Voice Health Tips

35 evidence-based tips across 5 categories with browser speech synthesis voice reading.

Location Awareness

GPS-based facility locator showing simulated nearest health posts, clinics, and hospitals.

QR Health Card

Real QR codes generated and downloaded as PNG — carrying your unique health ID and wellness reference.
